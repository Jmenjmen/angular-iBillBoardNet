DESCRIPTION

VitaCALCITE is a 100% natural plant growth supplement based on foliar application which delivers reliable supply of calcium (Ca), silicon (Si), and other trace elements (Mg, Zn, Cu, Fe, Mo) exactly where it is most needed – to the leaves and in a form readily recognizable for plants to build amino acids.

VitaCALCITE functions as an effective source of bioavailable calcium and orthosilicic acid; whereas calcium favors effective chloroplast protein transport and higher photosynthetic activity manifest in greater nutrient uptake and yield, silicon mitigates biotic and abiotic stressors and offers a rigid barrier in the face of pests, ensuring good health of your plant and preventing declining yields.

COMPOSITION

VitaCALCITE is derived from a natural deposit rock composed of calcite (calcium carbonate), quartz (silicon dioxide), and other calcite related minerals such as dolomite [magnesium calcium carbonate; (CaMg(CO₃)₂) and ankerite [calcium iron magnesium manganese carbonate; (CaM(CO₃)₂) where M = Mn, Zn, Fe].

It contains micronized natural calcium (average particle size of 5 ųm) produced through our advanced Enhanced Dynamic Activation (EDA) technology. Microbial size allows the water dissolved VitaCALCITE to be absorbed directly by target plant/crop where the various nutrients (Ca₂⁺, HCO₃⁻, Mg₂⁺, etc.) diffuse, resp. they reach the leaves inside cells.

APPLICATION & USAGE

VitaCALCITE is applied via foliar spray across the leaves of the plant as a 0,3% - 0,5% aqueous solution. This rate usually corresponds to 1,5 - 3kg of product mixed in approximately 200 to 400 liters of water (per ha).

  • Orchards and vineyards: 2 – 2,5 kg/ha

  • Vegetable gardens: 1,5 kg/ha

  • Smaller surfaces: 1 teaspoon (≈ 5g) in 1l of water

In general, the first application is deployed at the beginning of the vegetative period in leaf sprout (minimum of 2 leaves) and then every 10 - 14 days up to a total of 2 - 5 applications. Discontinue 1 month before the end of the growing season.
